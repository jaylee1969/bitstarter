Need an easier way to validate your choices while shopping? This mobile app will allow you to use your phone to scan product UPC codes with your phone and immediately confirm whether they align with your lifestyle choices.

Suppose you want to limit your family's consumption of MSG. Did you know that there are over 50 synonyms for this ingredient in food labeling? How can anyone reasonably keep track of this as they shop? This application provides a tool to make these decisions easy.

Participate in a community of users who care about what they're putting into their family's bodies. Search our vast database of product ingredients, and if you find something we aren't tracking yet, contribute your find easily for the benefit of others. Our database of products, with easily customizable watchlists enable you to traverse the maze of ingredients you want to highlight and avoid. We have pre-built watchlists that you can use immediately, and we also allow you to customize your own personal list of ingredients you'd like to avoid. Advanced functionality allows you to build a custom dietary weighting system which immediately grades a product as a good, fair, or poor dietary choice based on rules specific to you. These rules can then be contributed back to the community to share with others.
